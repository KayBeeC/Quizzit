How To Play

All users will be able to play a quiz. Just head to the Categories section and pick your quiz.

Each user will have a set time to play and complete a quiz. For unregistered users, the hardened difficult is locked. As well as this, your scores will not be added to the leaderboards, so register!

You will be able to see how well you did compared to others after completing the quiz.

Once you complete or attempt a quiz, you will not be able to play that quiz for another 24 hours.
